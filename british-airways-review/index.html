British Airways Review Dashboard

An interactive Tableau dashboard analyzing customer satisfaction for British Airways from 2016 to 2023.

The Challenge: Understanding the Voice of the Customer

British Airways, like any global airline, faces the challenge of meeting the diverse needs of its customers. Travelers leave reviews that reflect their experiences, from praises for attentive staff to complaints about uncomfortable seats or poor in-flight entertainment.

The Journey: Building the Dashboard

To tell the story of British Airways' customer satisfaction, I analyzed reviews from March 2016 to October 2023, focusing on:

  • Cabin staff service
  • Entertainment
  • Food and beverages
  • Seat comfort
  • Value for money
