British Airways Review Dashboard

Comprehensive customer feedback analysis for British Airways from March 2016 to October 2023.

Project Overview

This dashboard provides a comprehensive analysis of customer feedback and reviews for British Airways from March 2016 to October 2023. Built using Tableau, it visualizes customer ratings across various categories, such as overall satisfaction, cabin staff service, food, entertainment, and seat comfort.

The dashboard is designed to help stakeholders in the airline industry identify trends, strengths, and improvement opportunities by offering detailed insights into ratings by month, traveler type, region, and aircraft.

Project Objectives

  • Analyze overall and category-specific ratings to understand customer satisfaction.
  • Explore trends over time to identify seasonal variations or recurring challenges.
  • Compare geographic regions to pinpoint areas requiring targeted service improvements.
  • Evaluate aircraft performance to identify models that excel or need attention.
  • Empower stakeholders with interactive filters to customize and deep dive into specific metrics.

4. Average Overall Rating By Aircraft (Bar Chart)

A horizontal bar chart compares the average customer ratings for different aircraft types alongside the number of reviews for each:

  • Boeing 747-400 has the highest rating (4.7) but fewer reviews (97).
  • Boeing 777 has a solid rating (4.4) with the highest number of reviews (263).
  • A321 has the lowest rating (3.6), reflecting potential comfort or performance issues.

Insights:

  • Aircraft like the Boeing 747-400 and Boeing 787 consistently receive higher ratings, indicating positive customer experiences.
  • Aircraft like the A321 and A319 underperform, suggesting the need for further investigation into passenger dissatisfaction (e.g., seating arrangements, onboard features).

5. Interactive Filters

Interactive filters make the dashboard dynamic and user-friendly. You can view visualizations by choosing each metric separately:

  • Pick a Metric: Explore specific categories (overall rating, food, entertainment, seat comfort, etc.).
  • Time Range (March 2016 - October 2023): Adjust the date range to analyze trends over specific periods.
  • Traveler Type: Segment ratings by traveler type (e.g., business, couple leisure, family leisure, solo leisure).
  • Seat Type: Filter by ticket class (e.g., business class, economy class, premium economy, first class).
  • Aircraft Group: Focus on individual aircraft models to compare performance.
  • Continent: Narrow down ratings by region.

Key Findings

  • Entertainment and Food Are the Weakest Areas: Ratings of 1.4 and 2.4 highlight significant dissatisfaction in these areas.
  • Aircraft Performance: Boeing 747-400 and Boeing 777 excel in ratings, while A321 and A319 underperform.
  • Geographic Variations: Tailoring services to underperforming regions could improve satisfaction.
  • Seasonal Trends: Seasonal dips in ratings suggest challenges during peak times.
