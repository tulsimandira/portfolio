Customer Analysis Dashboard

A comprehensive and interactive analysis of customer purchasing behavior, revenue, and sales patterns.

Project Overview

The Customer Analysis Dashboard provides an in-depth look at customer purchasing patterns, sales trends, and revenue generation across various dimensions. Built using Tableau, it enables businesses to uncover actionable insights and make data-driven decisions.

This dashboard allows users to explore data across 15 categories: Appliances, Beauty & Grooming, Books, Computing, Entertainment, Health & Sports, Home & Living, Kids & Baby, Men’s Fashion, Mobiles & Tablets, Others, School & Education, Soghaat, Superstore, and Women’s Fashion.

Insights from the Dashboard

Month-Wise Revenue

The month-wise revenue trend highlights seasonal variations in sales performance. Peaks are observed in December, driven by holiday shopping, and April, likely due to spring promotions. Off-peak months like January and July provide opportunities to boost sales through targeted campaigns.

Revenue per State

A heatmap visualizes state-level revenue contributions. States like California, Texas, and New York show the highest revenue, while states such as Alaska and Vermont contribute less, indicating potential areas for market expansion.

Region-Wise Revenue Share

The pie chart reveals revenue distribution across regions. The South leads with 38.37% of total revenue, followed by the Midwest at 26.93%. The West and Northeast have lower contributions, highlighting areas to focus on for future growth.

Gender-Wise Sales Analysis

This chart divides sales revenue by gender and category. Female customers significantly drive revenue in Women’s Fashion and Beauty & Grooming, while male customers dominate Mobiles & Tablets and Appliances. Balanced contributions are seen in Books and School & Education.

Age-Wise Revenue

The age-wise revenue chart shows that customers aged 30-40 contribute the most revenue, followed by those aged 60-70. Younger customers (<20) and older customers (>70) have lower contributions, providing insights into demographic-specific marketing strategies.

Quantity-Discount Correlation

The scatter plot analyzes how discounts impact purchasing behavior. Higher discounts often correlate with increased quantities ordered, though excessive discounts show diminishing returns, highlighting the need for strategic pricing.

Interactive Features

The dashboard includes interactive filters that enable users to:

  • Analyze specific categories (e.g., Entertainment, Computing, Mobiles & Tablets).
  • Focus on specific demographics by filtering by gender, age group, or region.
  • Adjust the time range to identify seasonal patterns or trends over the years.
