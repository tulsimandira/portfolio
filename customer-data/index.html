Customer Analysis Dashboard

The Customer Analysis Dashboard provides a detailed and interactive view of customer purchasing behavior, revenue generation, and sales patterns across various dimensions. It showcases how businesses can derive actionable insights to understand performance better, identify growth opportunities, and refine strategies.

This dashboard analyzes customer behavior across categories, regions, genders, age groups, and time periods, with dynamic filters to explore specific segments. It answers crucial questions like:

  • Which product categories contribute the most to revenue?
  • How does revenue vary across regions and states?
  • What demographic segments are the primary contributors?
  • How do discounts affect quantity ordered?

Revenue by Categories

The dashboard breaks down revenue into 15 distinct product categories:

  • Appliances
  • Beauty & Grooming
  • Books
  • Computing
  • Entertainment
  • Health & Sports
  • Home & Living
  • Kids & Baby
  • Men’s Fashion
  • Mobiles & Tablets
  • Others
  • School & Education
  • Soghaat
  • Superstore
  • Women’s Fashion

Key insights include:

  • Mobiles & Tablets and Women’s Fashion dominate revenue.
  • Appliances, Beauty & Grooming, and Superstore also show strong contributions.
  • Categories like Books, Health & Sports, and Soghaat show potential for growth.

Revenue per State

The heatmap visualizes revenue distribution across states:

  • High-performing states include California, Texas, and New York.
  • Lower contributions are observed in states like Alaska and Vermont.

Monthly Revenue Trends

The Month-Wise Revenue chart tracks revenue trends over time:

  • Revenue spikes in December (holiday shopping) and April (spring promotions).
  • Lower revenue is observed in January and July, indicating off-peak seasons.

Region-Wise Revenue Share

The pie chart illustrates revenue contribution by region:

  • South: 38.37%
  • Midwest: 26.93%
  • West: 17.60%
  • Northeast: 17.10%

Gender-Wise Sales Analysis

The Gender-Wise Sales chart highlights revenue contributions by gender:

  • Female customers dominate revenue in Women’s Fashion and Beauty & Grooming.
  • Male customers lead in Mobiles & Tablets and Appliances.
  • Categories like Books and School & Education show balanced contributions.

Age-Wise Revenue Analysis

The Age-Wise Revenue chart explains:

  • The 30-40 age group generates the highest revenue ($44.8M).
  • The 60-70 age group follows closely with $41.1M.
  • Young customers (<20 years) and older customers (>70 years) contribute the least.

Quantity-Discount Correlation

The scatter plot shows:

  • Higher discounts generally lead to larger quantities being ordered.
  • Excessive discounts show diminishing returns, emphasizing the need for strategic pricing.

Interactive Features

  • Filter by category, gender, age group, and region for detailed analysis.
  • Adjust time range to explore seasonal trends or specific years.
