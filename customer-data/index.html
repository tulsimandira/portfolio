Customer Analysis Dashboard

Interactive insights into customer purchasing behavior, revenue trends, and sales patterns.

The Customer Analysis Dashboard provides a detailed and interactive view of customer purchasing behavior, revenue generation, and sales patterns across various dimensions. This dashboard helps businesses understand their performance better, identify growth opportunities, and refine strategies.

It offers analysis across different categories, regions, genders, age groups, and time periods. Users can answer critical questions such as:

  • Which product categories contribute the most to revenue?
  • How does revenue vary across regions and states?
  • What demographic segments are the primary contributors?
  • How do discounts affect quantity ordered?

Revenue per State

A heatmap visualizes sales distribution across the United States. States like California, Texas, and New York consistently generate high revenue, while smaller contributions are observed in less populous states such as Alaska and Vermont.

Monthly Revenue Trends

A line chart tracks sales performance over time, highlighting peaks during December (holiday shopping) and April (spring promotions). Lower revenue periods like January and July indicate off-peak seasons.

Region-Wise Revenue Share

A pie chart shows the revenue split by region. The South leads with 38.37% of total revenue, followed by the Midwest (26.93%), West (17.60%), and Northeast (17.10%).

Gender-Wise Sales Analysis

A bar chart divides revenue by gender across categories. For example, female customers dominate in Women’s Fashion ($15.04M) and Beauty & Grooming ($1.37M), while male customers lead in Mobiles & Tablets ($66.27M) and Appliances ($13.45M).

Age-Wise Revenue Analysis

A bar chart shows revenue by age group. The 30-40 age group contributes the most ($44.8M), while the <20 group contributes the least ($7M).

Quantity-Discount Correlation

A scatter plot analyzes how discounts impact the quantity ordered. It shows that higher discounts often increase order quantities, but extreme discounts may result in diminishing returns.

Interactive Features

The dashboard includes filters for categories, time ranges, gender, age groups, and regions, allowing users to uncover tailored insights.
